# Какие фразы использовать на техническом собеседовании на английском?

## Ответ

Рассказ о себе и опыте:
```
I have 2 years of experience with React and Node.js.
I'm currently working as a frontend developer at...
My main responsibilities include...
```

Обсуждение задачи:
```
Could you clarify the requirements?
Let me walk you through my approach.
I'd start by breaking this down into smaller steps.
What's the expected input/output here?
```

Если не знаешь ответ — честно, но конструктивно:
```
I haven't worked with that directly, but I understand the concept as...
I'm not 100% sure, but my guess would be...
Could I look that up, or should I reason through it?
```

Вопросы к интервьюеру в конце:
```
What does the tech stack look like?
What's the team structure?
What would success look like in this role after 3 months?
```
