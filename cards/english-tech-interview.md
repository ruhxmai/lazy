# Какие фразы помогут на техническом интервью по-английски?
## Ответ
**Когда нужно подумать:**
- "Let me think about this for a moment..."
- "That's an interesting question. My first thought is..."

**Объясняешь подход:**
- "My approach would be to..."
- "I'd start by... and then..."
- "The trade-off here is speed vs. memory."

**Если не знаешь ответ:**
- "I haven't worked with that directly, but I'd approach it by..."
- "I'm not 100% sure, but I believe..."

**Уточняешь условие задачи:**
- "Just to clarify — should I optimize for time or space complexity?"
- "Can I assume the input is always valid?"
- "What's the expected scale of the data?"
