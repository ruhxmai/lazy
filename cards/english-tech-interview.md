# Какие фразы помогут на техническом собеседовании на английском?

## Ответ

**Уточнить задачу перед решением:**
- "Could you clarify what you mean by...?"
- "Just to make sure I understand — you want me to..., right?"
- "Can I ask a clarifying question before I start?"

**Думать вслух (важно на coding interview):**
- "Let me think through this step by step."
- "My first approach would be to..."
- "I'm considering two options here: ... and ..."
- "The time complexity of this would be O(n)."

**Когда не знаешь ответа:**
- "I'm not sure about the exact syntax, but the idea is..."
- "I haven't worked with that before, but I would approach it by..."
- "That's outside my current knowledge — could you give me a hint?"

**Обсуждение трейд-оффов:**
- "The advantage of this approach is... however, the downside is..."
- "This is more readable, but less performant."
- "It depends on the use case — if ... then ..., otherwise ..."

**Завершить мысль:**
- "Does that make sense?"
- "Would that work for your use case?"
